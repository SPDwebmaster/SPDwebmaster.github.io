FALL RUSH 2022

What is Rush?!

Rush week is a series of events we put on so you can learn more about Sigma Phi Delta. Our next rush will be week 2 of Fall 2022. Stay tuned for event details!

Monday 9/26: BBQ

  • Time: 6pm at Dexter Lawn
  • Come get free Pizza, meet the bros, and learn more about Sigma Phi Delta.

Tuesday 9/27: Game Night @ The Island (Meet @ the PAC for rides)

  • Time: 6pm, meet at PAC
  • Come play games, hang out with us, and have fun at one of our fraternity homes.

Wednesday 9/28: Bowling @ Mustang Lanes

  • Time: 6pm, meet at Mustang Lanes
  • Come play bowling with us and eat pizza, for free. Show off your skills or lack of skills, we don't judge.

Thursday 9/29: Professional day @ The Castle (Meet @ the PAC for rides)

  • Time: 6pm, meet at PAC
  • As we are a professional engineering fraternity, we have many brothers and alumni working in industry. This night is a career fair style professional day to share what we've learned and how we can help you in your professional career.

Friday and Saturday: Invite only events

  • We will be inviting rushees back to go through the next stages of our process.
