WINTER RUSH 2020

Monday 1/13: Kickoff Barbecue

  • Time: 6pm
  • Location: Sierra Madre
  • Begin Rush Week with burgers and dogs at the Sierra Madre Barbeque. This is an opportunity to learn all about Sigma Phi Delta and see what being a brother is all about.

Tuesday 1/14: Bowling at Mustang Lanes

  • Time: 6pm
  • Location: University Union
  • Come to Mustang Lanes for some free bowling and pizza. Show us what you got!

Wednesday 1/15: Tech Talks at the Castle

  • Time: 6pm
  • Location: Meet at the PAC Circle for rides
  • The Castle is the symbol of our fraternity, as well as the name of our house. We will be presenting tech talks on various projects our brothers have been undertaking.

Thursday 1/16: Night at Hope Street

  • Time: 6pm
  • Location: Meet at the PAC Circle for rides
  • Smash Bros, VR, and pizza at one of our sattelite houses. This is your final chance to meet some brothers and demonstrate your interest in the fraternity.

Friday and Saturday: Invite only events

  • Rushees who have shown interest through participation in earlier rush event will be invited to these weekend events.

Please direct any questions to Recruitment Chairman Ryan Powell: recruitment.spd.calpoly@gmail.com
